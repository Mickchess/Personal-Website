What is Food Ai?

Food Ai is a web application that uses machine learning to identify food items in an image. Simply upload a photo of your meal and Food Ai will tell you what's on your plate!

How does it work?

Food Ai uses a pre-trained model to recognize food items in an image. The model has been trained on a large dataset of food images and can identify hundreds of different food items.

Why use Food Ai?

Food Ai is a fun and easy way to learn more about the food you eat. Whether you're trying to count calories, track your macros, or just curious about what's in your meal, Food Ai can help you make more informed choices about your diet.
